FAQ

Frequently asked questions

  • What is Youthfuly?

    youthfully is an initiative created by a group of moroccan youth full of passion,and motivation to develop youth's skills in a variety of fields. While also promoting social change, through providing opportunities for youth to improve their talents and learn new skills. and most importantly giving youth the chance to share their voices and plan various activities.

  • What is Youthfuly's goal?

    Youthfuly's goal is to inspire people especially youth to think out of the box and be more open to learn and experience new things, by offering a variety of workshops and events.

  • Why did Youthfuly start?

    Youthfuly started due to the lack of easy found & understood information recourses.

    It is a place where everyone can find reliable informations that will for sure help him/her to improve him/her self.
